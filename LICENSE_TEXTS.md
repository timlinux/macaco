## Usage in MoCaCo

MoCaCo uses short excerpts (sentences) from these works as sample text for vim motion training exercises. The texts are used to generate procedural training tasks that help users practice vim commands.

No modifications have been made to the original text content beyond selecting individual sentences for use in training exercises.
